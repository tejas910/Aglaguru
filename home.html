Why use Aglaguru

DISCOVERABILITY

Finding a teacher is not tough but finding the right one for you is, AglaGuru is here to help you find the best teachers in your city and around the country

PAYMENT SECURITY

AglaGuru has come with an innovative payment system where when you buy learning hours from the app, you can claim a refund on the remaining classes in case you are not satisfied with the teacher or any other dissatisfactory circumstances

TRIAL CLASSES

Aglaguru will provide you with free trial classes which will enable to you to understand if any particular teacher is right for you before finalizing your tuition sessions with that teacher

Why we created Aglaguru?

“We believe every hour spent with the teacher is an INVESTMENT made towards achieving better results”

One of the major problems faced by students is finding an appropriate teacher for themselves and it was the same in our case too, we would spend days in finding a teacher and we only had two option either through somebodies reference or through newspapers which was not only a cumbersome process but also very inefficient, because we never had an opportunity to take trial classes with the teacher before starting our session so we never knew if we would be comfortable with the teacher’s teaching style and mostly ended up with the wrong teacher which wasted our valueble time.

So. AglaGuru is here to eliminate these inefficiencies that currently exist and improve the overall tuition experience for both teachers & students
